My Favorite Hobby is Cricket

My hobby is Cricket. I love to go outside in the beautiful weather and swing the bat or throw the ball. All though it may take a lot of time to finish one game, it is a very fun and strategic game. Cricket can give you good excercise, and you cna play it if you are bored. I recommend you to play it outside and not indoors. But only go outside if there is good weather. You need to have good catching skills, good throwing skills so you can hit wicket, and good batting skills ot hit the ball and achieve a six or a four. You also need to look carefully where the ball is so you can actually hit it. These are the reasons why Cricket is my favorite hobby.
